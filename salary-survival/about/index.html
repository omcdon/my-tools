About Salary Survival

What Is Salary Survival?

Salary Survival is a free salary vs cost of living calculator designed for Americans who want to understand their real financial situation. We go beyond gross income to show you what actually matters: your take-home pay after taxes, your monthly expenses, and whether you can genuinely afford to live where you are.

How We Calculate

  1. 1. Gross Income: Your annual salary or hourly wage × 2,080 hours.
  2. 2. Federal Tax: Calculated using 2026 federal income tax brackets with standard deduction.
  3. 3. State Tax: Each state's income tax brackets are applied. States with no income tax show $0.
  4. 4. FICA: Social Security (6.2% up to $176,100) + Medicare (1.45%) + Additional Medicare (0.9% over $200k).
  5. 5. Expenses: Rent (state average for 1BR), groceries, transportation, utilities, healthcare, debt, and car payment.
  6. 6. Affordability Score: Based on savings rate (30%), rent-to-income ratio (20%), and debt-to-income ratio (20%).

Data Sources

  • Federal tax brackets: IRS 2026 guidelines
  • State tax brackets: State Department of Revenue publications
  • Cost of living data: Bureau of Labor Statistics, Census Bureau, and rental market reports
  • Income percentiles: US Census Bureau Current Population Survey
