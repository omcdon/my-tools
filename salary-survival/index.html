Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the salary calculator?
Our calculator uses 2026 federal and state tax brackets, plus average cost-of-living data from government sources. It provides a good estimate, but actual amounts may vary based on deductions, credits, and specific city costs.
Does this calculator include all tax deductions?
We use the standard deduction for simplicity. Itemized deductions, tax credits, and pre-tax contributions (like 401k) are not included. For a precise tax calculation, consult a tax professional.
What does the Affordability Score mean?
The score (0-100) evaluates your financial health based on savings rate, rent burden, and debt-to-income ratio. 70+ is comfortable, 40-70 is manageable, below 40 means financial stress.
Can I compare salaries between states?
Yes! Our state salary pages show detailed breakdowns for any salary in any state, including comparisons with cheaper alternatives. Try the links on the homepage.
