How to Use This Tool 使用说明

  1. Type or paste your text into the input area.
  2. The tool instantly displays: character count, word count, sentence count, paragraph count, Chinese character count, English character count, digit count, and estimated reading time.
  3. Click Copy Stats to copy all statistics to your clipboard.
  4. Works with mixed Chinese and English text — Chinese characters are counted individually, English words are counted by spaces.
  5. This is useful for writers, students, social media posters, and anyone who needs to meet character limits.

Frequently Asked Questions 常见问题

  • How is reading time calculated? — Reading time is estimated based on 200 words per minute for English text and 300 characters per minute for Chinese text, which are standard average reading speeds.
  • Does it count spaces and punctuation? — Characters include all visible characters (letters, numbers, punctuation, spaces). Word count only counts words (groups of characters separated by spaces).
  • What is the difference between characters and words? — Character count counts every individual character including spaces. Word count counts groups of characters separated by whitespace. A 500-character text in English might have around 80-100 words.
  • 阅读时间是怎么计算的？ — 阅读时间基于英文每分钟 200 个词、中文每分钟 300 个字符的标准平均阅读速度估算。
  • 空格和标点算吗？ — 字符数包含所有可见字符（字母、数字、标点、空格）。单词数只计算单词（由空格分隔的字符组）。
  • 字符数和单词数有什么区别？ — 字符数计算每个单独的字符（包括空格）。单词数计算由空格分隔的字符组。500 个英文字符大约是 80-100 个单词。
