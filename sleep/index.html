  5. Select the time that best fits your schedule for optimal rest.

Frequently Asked Questions 常见问题

  • What is a sleep cycle? — A sleep cycle lasts approximately 90 minutes and consists of stages: light sleep, deep sleep, and REM sleep. Completing full cycles (rather than waking up in the middle of one) helps you feel more refreshed.
  • How many cycles do I need? — Most adults need 5-6 complete sleep cycles per night, which equals 7.5 to 9 hours of sleep. The exact number varies by individual.
  • Why do I still feel tired after enough sleep? — If you wake up in the middle of a deep sleep stage, you may feel groggy regardless of total sleep time. This calculator aims to help you wake up between cycles for a more refreshed feeling.
  • 什么是睡眠周期？ — 一个睡眠周期约 90 分钟，包含浅睡、深睡和 REM 睡眠阶段。完成完整的周期（而非在周期中间醒来）有助于让你感觉更清醒。
  • 我需要多少个周期？ — 大多数成人每晚需要 5-6 个完整睡眠周期，即 7.5 到 9 小时。具体数量因人而异。
  • 为什么睡够了还是累？ — 如果你在深睡阶段中间醒来，可能仍会感到昏沉，与总睡眠时间无关。此计算器旨在帮助你在周期之间醒来，获得更清爽的感觉。
