Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Freemaki ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit freemaki.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Tool Data: All calculations and conversions are performed entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools (such as text, numbers, colors, or personal measurements).

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our Site, third-party services may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral source
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. Cookies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: To remember your language preference (English/Chinese). These are set by our own JavaScript and do not require third-party consent.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Site. This data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense and its partners may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. These are third-party cookies.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Site.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements on our Site. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the Site. You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on to disable tracking.
  • Google Fonts: Loads web fonts for displaying text. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are loaded.

4. Advertising

We display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and browsing behavior. Google's advertising privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. Advertisers receive aggregated, non-personally-identifiable data.

5. Data Security

Since all tool processing happens locally in your browser, your input data never leaves your device. Our Site is served over HTTPS to ensure secure communication. We regularly review our security practices but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
