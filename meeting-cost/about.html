About This Calculator

This calculator uses fully-loaded employee costs (salary + benefits + overhead) to estimate meeting burn rate. All calculations happen in your browser—no data is stored or transmitted.

The Formula

Total Cost =

(Number of Attendees × Average Annual Cost ÷ 2,080 work hours/year) × Meeting Duration in hours

Why 2,080 Hours?

2,080 is the standard number of working hours per year: 40 hours per week × 52 weeks. This is a common HR benchmark for calculating hourly rates from annual salaries.

What is Fully-Loaded Cost?

The fully-loaded cost of an employee includes:

  • Gross salary or wage
  • Health insurance premiums
  • Retirement plan contributions (401k match, etc.)
  • Payroll taxes (Social Security, Medicare, FUTA, SUTA)
  • Paid time off (vacation, sick days, holidays)
  • Workers' compensation insurance
  • Office space, equipment, and software
  • Training and professional development

A common rule of thumb is that the fully-loaded cost is 1.3 to 1.4 times the gross salary.

Privacy

This tool is designed with privacy in mind. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. All calculations run locally in your browser using JavaScript. The page works offline after the first load.

Fun Conversions

The fun conversions (coffee, Netflix, etc.) use approximate US prices as of 2025. They're meant to put meeting costs in relatable terms—not to be precisely accurate for any given location or time.
